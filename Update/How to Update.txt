How to update Radio Portal from 1.0/1.1 to 1.2

Please follow the steps below to update.

1. [Important] Take a backup of your database and all files before starting the update.
2. Upload update.php to your site's home folder.
3. Run yoursite.com/update.php on your browser
4. Follow the instructions.
5. Delete the update.php

If you have experienced any problems, please contact us at support@foxart.co (not .com)
